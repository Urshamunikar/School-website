यस क्षेत्रका हरेक परिवारका बाल बालिबालिकाले सहुलियत शूल्कमा  अंग्रेजी माध्यममा गुणस्तरीय शिक्षा लिन पाउन् भन्ने उद्देश्यले वि.सं. २०४६ साल पूष १० गते स्थानीय शिक्षाप्रेमी श्रीमती सपना दुलालले का.म.न.पा. वडा नं ७ मैजूबहालमा यो विद्यालय स्थापना गर्नुभएको हो । पहिले प्रा .वि पछि नि.मा.वि हुदै माध्यमिक भएको यो विद्यालयले आफ्नो स्थापनाकालको  ३० वर्ष पूरा गरिसक्दा  २०औं ब्याचसम्म एस एल सी परीक्षामा सफलतापुर्वक सम्मिलित भई कैयौं दक्ष नागरिक उत्पादन गरिसकेको कुरा अवगत गराउँन चाहन्छौ । 

समयसँगै केही व्यवस्थापकीय परिवर्तन भई यस विद्यालयमा पहिले देखिनै अध्यापन गराउदै आउनुभएका शिक्षकहरु  श्री दिलिप आचार्य , श्री विनोद सिटौलालगायतको समूहलेनै २०७२ साल वैशाखदेखि यसको  ब्यवस्थापन जिम्मा लिई  अरु स्तरीय र समयसापेक्ष बनाउने अठोटका साथ अघि बढिरहेको पनि सबैलाई जानकारी गराउँदछौं । समयको माग अनुसार उपयोगी  शिक्षा प्रदान गर्न कुनै पनि सम्झौता नगर्ने बिचार लिई  २०७२ साल फागुन २० देखि  का.म.न.पा वडा नं ७ खरीबोट नजिकै कुमारीगालस्थित भुकम्प प्रतिरोधी भवनमा सरिसकेको यो विद्यालयमा हाल करिब ४२० विद्यार्थी अध्ययनरत छन् । तीस जना शिक्षक तथा कर्मचारी रहेको यस विद्यालयमा प्राइमरी ब्लकमा प्लेग्रुपदेखि कक्षा तीन सम्म र अर्को ब्लकमा चार देखि दश कक्षा सम्म पढाइ हुने गर्दछ । घोकन्ते विद्याभन्दा पनि  ब्यबहारिक शिक्षामा बढी जोड दिई नैतिक शिक्षा पनि प्रदान गर्दै विद्यार्थीलाई अझ अनुशासित र दक्ष बनाउनु यो विद्यालयको मूख्य उद्देश्य हो ।

Introduction

Prerana English Boarding High School is an educational community where students and staff of various nationalities, religious and background live, study and work together in a congenial atmosphere. PEBHS was established in 2046 BS by Mrs. Sapana Dulal with the help of some well wishers. ( From 2072 BS its management is smoothly run by  Chair Person Mr. Dilip Achraya  and Principal Mr Vinod Sitaula  with the help of a team of well qualified teachers) . It was established with the aim of enhancing the level of education of lower and middle class students by providing them quality education at affordable fees. In the history of more than two decades PEBHS has established itself as a leading school in this locality by producing hundreds of students able to match the need of the country and the age as well. Along with the knowledge of course books, PEBHS is offering students ample opportunities through various activities to discover, uplift and refine their inner talents. This school has highly dedicated qualified and competent teaching staffs who believe in persuasion to promote spiritual, moral cultural, mental and physical development of the children and to build up self-confidence in them.

Aims and Objectives

Our aim is to provide an all round education to boys and girls from different areas of the country and from all social and economic background in which children are prepared to face any crisis in life with normal dignity and to become useful and loyal citizen of their country. This school is always committed and devoted to meet the need of the students for their all-round development. In addition following are the objectives:

*           To impart sound foundation of knowledge in English, Mathematics, Science           and so on.

*           To make the children creative, thoughtful and more responsive towards their          competitive future.

*           Plenty of emphasis is laid on debates, poem and quiz competition, games, co-     curricular and extracurricular activities with equal opportunities for         participation by all students with a view of their physical, mental and spiritual             development.
