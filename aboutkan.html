• Introduction:

                      Kantipur English Academy School (KEA School) established in 2000 AD is an educational co-operative community where students and staffs of various religion, nationalities and background live, study, create and work together in a congenial atmosphere. It is a co-educational institute situated in a peaceful locality with motorable road of Gongabu beside Mukteshwor temple near Ring Road between the new bus park and Gongabu police bit. It is an ideal place for educational activities and residence. We believe that the perfect education can only be imparted when the school, society and home work in tandem in a spirit of mutual respect. The school will provide a comprehensive system of education to enhance them to be creative, analytical and problem solver. The school intends to provide student centered, result oriented, education in a hygienic and congenial environment by a team of professionally trained, dedicated and motivated teachers, lovingly serving Aayas and a peaceful area of calm locality and the best hostel.

                             The main aim is to provide all round education in which children are prepared to face any crisis in life with moral dignity and to become useful and loyal citizens of the country. Special emphasis is laid to creative outlets and extra co-curricular activities along with the infusion of pure and sound knowledge through scientific, creative and modern methods. We pursue for excellence by encompassing the objective to learning by doing.

                               We believe to develop each child’s potential individually as well as carter to their basic and individual needs with constant monitoring innovative back up, evaluation and support to be well balanced citizens with a healthy attitude towards life.

     

    Parent/ Guardian’s – School relationship:

                      School education is a joint enterprise involving the school, students and parents. We believe in co-operative relation as such challenging task cannot be achieved by us alone without a helping hand from the parents. Healthy and close link between the parents and school is the form of free, frank and frequent dialogue would bridge the gap to have a good influence to all aspects of a child’s development. We can achieve our goal only when there would be an active participation of the parents in the form of valuable suggestions, advices and contractive criticism.

     

    Language policy:

                       English is the medium of instruction, as fluent English is usually a basic requirements for admission to centers of higher education. It is easy means of communication in the international sphere of life. It is a language, which can be easily understood in different parts of the world.

     

    Staff:

                 Kantipur has tried to search a highly qualified and competent teaching staff. The school employs other staff to serve the children as their mother does. The different classes of the school are under direct control of the principal within it the divisions of different sections and in charge ship headed to various head teachers to watch them individually.

     

    School Functions:   

                   The school holds functions on special occasions. The annual award distribution day, parents / teachers discussions day, inter school competitions and a number of cultural celebration and rally in the academic sessions.

     

    Curriculum:

                   The teaching curriculum includes very modern and highly competent books selecting from various publications. We believe different writers gives distinct methods and change every year the publications textbooks so as to give everyone’s style comparatively.

     

  • Admission:

                  Admission will be granted for both boys and girls before the beginning of the new syllabus. Those who are willing to admit their child can obtain admission forms and prospectus from the account office on payment of Rs.100/. Parents are requested to submit their wards birth certificate, health certificate and a transfer certificate if the child has formerly attended another school. Anyway the principal before the admission will take final decision.

    Examination:

                 The school is divided in 3 Mid Terminals Examinations one after another. Other class tests and monthly tests are conducted as the subject teacher’s convenience. The examination is scheduled as 1st  Mid Term> 1st Term, 2nd Mid Term> 2nd Term, 3rd Mid Term> 3rd Term.

                     Progress Report card will be provided after every Terms.

     

    School Timing:

                  The classes will go on all working days from Sunday to Friday from 9:15 AM to 3:20 PM in the junior section and 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM in the senior section. Children must arrive 15 minutes before the bell rings. Children reach after bell rings are returned to back home. Classes Nursery to Upper KG will be counted as junior classes and one onwards is senior one.

     

    Promotion:

     

                      Children failing to secure 50 percent in the total of 100 percent or failing in two of the major subjects like Math, English, Nepali, Science, Social studies etc shall be detained and will have to repeat the same class. Children who fail the class will be asked to take out from the school. Anyway children’s overall activities will be the reference for the promotion. 

  • Payment Rules:

                      Monthly charges includes tuition fee and bus fee (optional), which are charged for 12 months.

                      Fees are to be paid in advance by 10th of each month. Late fee is charged Rs. 10, 20, 30 respectively in every successive month. They will not be accepted if 3 months due is remain to clear.

     

    Books, Stationary and Educational Material:

                    The pre-primary section shall be provided all the stationaries to use in school and the exercise notebooks will be taken to do their home assignment too. Textbooks will be provided on payment of the market price from the school. Primary, Lower Secondary and Secondary Level children get exercise note books free from the school and other stationeries they have to carry from their home. The textbooks will be provided as above-mentioned way.

    Withdrawal:

                  Enrolled students can be withdrawn from the school only on a month’s notice prior to start the new session. However a student will be rusticated from the school if he/she is engaged in bad work or become more violator of the school environment.

     

    Complaints:
